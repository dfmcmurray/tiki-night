SWEET! HOLLYWOOD

Tiki Night 2013 will be located in the heart of Hollywood. Because this is a high pedestrian traffic area we ask that you please be extremely courteous to everyone around you while coming and going from our event. We could never raise funds for the National MS Society without the cooperation of Sweet! Hollywood, Hollywood & Highland, and all of you.

PARK IT!

Parking for Sweet! is in the parking structure underneath the Hollywood & Highland Center. There are 2 entrances to the structure, both between Hollywood Boulevard and Franklin Avenue:

  1. Highland Avenue (east of the Center)
  2. Orange Drive (west of the Center)
