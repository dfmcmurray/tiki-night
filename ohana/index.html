Our Ohana

This is how we do Tiki Night

After undergoing a nasty operation, Skypp decided he wanted to do something for the community that could incorporate his love for cycling. After doing a bit of research he found the National MS Society and the Bike MS Bay to Bay Tour. In order to ride in the event, Skypp needed to raise money for the National MS Society. That became his goal. Since that first year friends, family and co-workers have come out to him with their admission to having MS or knowing someone who has this complicated disease. It's for them that Skypp continues to raise funds for this charity and cycles the 150 miles year after year. It's for them that this fundraising party known as Tiki Night has been organised by Skypp year after year.

He can't do this alone...
