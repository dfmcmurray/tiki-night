What you need to know before you get to Tiki Night

2013 Ticket Prices

The basic admission donation ticket includes entry, light food, entertainment, and one entry for the door prize. There are other admission packages that include more drink tickets and raffle tickets. Additional beverages and raffle tickets will be available at the event for purchase or you can choose the admission package that suits you best when you buy your tickets in advance and on line. Expanded Tiki Night packages will not be sold in person or on the day of the party.

BIG KAHUNA SPONSORSHIP

For $400 you can sponsor part of our fundraising efforts at Tiki Night. Your sponsorship money will be used for our expenses - everything from food/drink supplies down helping us pay for the venue expenses. Anything left over will be given directly to the National MS Society.

In doing this, you'll be helping us to reduce our expenses and allowing us to send more donation money to the National MS Society.

For your sponsorship you will receive the following:

  • Naming rights and signage for one of the following: Our Stage, Our Food Station, or Our Tiki Bar.
  • 2 admissions, Light Food, 2 Drink Tickets, 2 Raffle Tickets, 2 entries for our Door Prize, 2 Official Team Tiki Ha Ha Candy Bars, 2 Party Leis, and a "surprise" party favor.
  • A Behind The Glass Tour at Sweet! Hollywood - see them make candy!
  • Exclusive seating are for you and your guest.
  • Signage and mentions during the event evening.
  • Logo and link placement on this web site.
  • Social Media exposure leading up to Tiki Night.
  • Marketing Materials or party favors placed in our Swag Bag for our guests' exposure.
  • Full color souvenir book from the evening (delivered via mail after the event).

HOW CAN I PAY FOR STUFF?

Tiki Night accepts Cash, Visa, MasterCards, American Express, and Discover Card. Advance ticket sales through Sold Out Crowd accepts the same. We encourage you to purchase your tickets in advance and on the web so that you are guaranteed a ticket for the event. It'll also help us figure out how much food, drink, and supplies to order prior to your arrival. Heaven forbid we run out of anything!

BEHAVE YOURSELF

We will continue to stress that this is a business area and we need to respect the other guests, shoppers, tourists, and businesses located around us. It is partially these people who are allowing us to do this event for the National MS Society.

ACCESSIBILITY

WE HEARD YOU LOUD AND CLEAR. Tiki Night 2013 has become accessible for those who need it! Got a wheelchair? No problem! We request that you please check in with Hollywood & Highland Center for more information.
